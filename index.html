About GDG

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are global communities of developers who come together to share knowledge, collaborate, and grow their skills in Google technologies. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, GDG events provide a platform to learn, network, and innovate.

At GDG Event 2025, you'll have the opportunity to:

  • Attend insightful talks and workshops led by industry experts.
  • Explore the latest trends in technology, including AI, Cloud, and Mobile Development.
  • Network with like-minded developers and professionals.
  • Participate in hands-on coding sessions and hackathons.

Community

Be part of a vibrant community of developers who share a passion for technology and innovation.

Learning

Gain insights from industry leaders and enhance your skills through hands-on workshops and sessions.

Networking

Connect with like-minded professionals and build lasting relationships in the tech industry.
